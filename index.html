Reply Studio @BraRicky

Paste a tweet, pick a mood, get a reply in your voice. Copy and post.

Voice rules (do's & don'ts) ›

Calm, grounded, elder-brother wisdom. Building something real, never the loud "alpha." Short, declarative, quotable.

Do

  • Be specific to the actual tweet.
  • Keep it short and quotable. One clean idea.
  • Speak to money pressure with respect, not hype.

Don't

  • No generic "Goodluck to everyone" lines.
  • No fake slang, no hashtags, no spam emojis.
  • No bragging or bitterness.
How to use & tips ›
  • Paste a tweet, pick one mood, tap Write replies.
  • You'll get a few options. Tap Copy on the best one and paste it into the reply box on X.
  • Want different ones? Tap Write more options.
  • First time only: you may see a quick Puter sign-in popup (free) - that's what powers the AI. Approve it once.
  • Keep it handy: open in your browser, then "Add to Home Screen."
